    Introduction to The Department

    The Department of Basic Sciences is a supportive and basic department for the departments of the Higher Institute of Engineering and Technology in New Minya. Its importance lies in providing students with a round of scientific and linguistic knowledge that they acquire from basic science courses (mathematics - physics - chemistry - foreign languages - computer).
